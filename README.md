🚀 Overview

Chandravanshi E-Scooty Management System is a web-based business management application designed to manage E-Scooty inventory, sales records, profit tracking, and monthly business reports.

The system allows users to add E-Scooty entries with their actual purchase price and selling price. Profit is calculated automatically and displayed in reports. Users can monitor monthly sales performance, total profit, and inventory records through an easy-to-use dashboard.

✨ Features
📦 E-Scooty Management
Add new E-Scooty entries
Store scooter details
Record actual purchase price
Record selling price
